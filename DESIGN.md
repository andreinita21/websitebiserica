# Design System Documentation

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system, reflecting the current theme settings.

## 1. Color Palette

Our color palette is crafted to convey a luxurious and sophisticated feel, anchored by a deep dark mode.

*   **Color Mode**: `dark` - The system operates primarily in a dark aesthetic, providing a rich backdrop for content.
*   **Primary Color**: `#D4AF37` (Goldenrod) - A rich, vibrant gold that signifies premium quality and acts as the primary accent for interactive elements.
*   **Secondary Color**: `#FABC02` (Bright Gold) - A brighter gold offering a supporting accent, suitable for secondary actions or highlights.
*   **Tertiary Color**: `#FFF8E5` (Creamy White) - A very light, almost off-white shade used for subtle accents and contrasts against the darker tones.
*   **Neutral Color**: `#121212` (Deep Charcoal) - The foundational dark color, providing depth and consistency across backgrounds and surfaces.

## 2. Typography

The typography strategy balances classic elegance with modern readability, ensuring a clear information hierarchy.

*   **Headline Font**: `notoSerif` - Chosen for its classic, elegant serifs, providing a strong and sophisticated presence for all headlines.
*   **Body Font**: `notoSerif` - Consistent with headlines, using `notoSerif` for body text maintains a cohesive and refined textual experience.
*   **Label Font**: `publicSans` - A clean, highly legible sans-serif font used for labels and smaller interface text, ensuring clarity and contrast with the serif fonts.

## 3. Shape and Form

The system incorporates a subtle approach to shape, balancing contemporary aesthetics with functional design.

*   **Roundedness**: `1` (Subtle) - Elements feature a hint of roundedness at the corners, softening the appearance without losing definition. This value provides a gentle touch to UI components.

## 4. Spacing

The spacing system is designed to provide clear separation and readability, ensuring a comfortable user experience without feeling overly dense or sparse.

*   **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - This setting ensures a balanced distribution of whitespace, creating a structured and comfortable layout that is neither too compact nor excessively open.
